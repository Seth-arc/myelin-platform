Section_01 // The Core Philosophy

Failure is
Non-Fatal.

In heavy industry, the cost of a mistake is measured in lives and millions in downtime. Myelin TMS is an AI-driven simulation platform architected to provide a "failure-safe" environment. Here, South African engineering trade learners master the **Lockout/Tagout (LOTO)** 7-step protocol through recursive, high-fidelity practice, bridging the gap between classroom theory and real-world QCTO-registered qualifications.
